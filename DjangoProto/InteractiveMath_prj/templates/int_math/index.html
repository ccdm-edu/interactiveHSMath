Interactable Math:

High School Algebra and Geometry

This website is designed to tie the "boring" stuff that high school students "have" to learn in Algebra 1 and 2 and Geometry to the real world in a way that is fun and interactive. We tie trig functions to music, imaginary numbers to cell phones, geometry to drones. Look through the tabs, you'll see it in action.
