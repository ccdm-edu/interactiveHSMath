Interactable Math:

High School Algebra, Geometry and Advanced Math

This website is designed to connect the "boring" stuff that high school students "have" to learn in Algebra 2 and Geometry and Advanced Math Topics to the real world in a way that is fun and interactive. You will see how trig functions play into music and imaginary numbers help your cell phone send your text messages (and do everything else phones do).
