Interactive Math:

High School Algebra and Geometry

In tutoring high school students for several years, I have found many who have complained that they have to "memorize all these stupid, useless equations". As an engineer, I want to explain how these "stupid" equations are found through out the their world and are the building blocks of technology. This site is designed to give high schoolers a taste of what college students learn in a fun and interactive way.

This site is intended to cover Algebra 2 and Geometry, eventually. Only one person is writing code for this site so progress is slow. As of now, it is tested only on Chrome web browser and generally works under Firefox and is flaky under Safari. It looks best on a laptop/desktop/notebook, untested on mobile devices. Enjoy!
